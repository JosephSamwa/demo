Vision

To be a leading global source of excellent tea, building a legacy of sustainability, creativity, and unrivaled quality that enhances the tea-drinking experience for tea lovers worldwide.

Mission

We aspire to produce great teas in a socially and economically responsible manner, focusing on sustainability, fair trade, and community empowerment to benefit both tea lovers and local communities.

Core Values

  • • Quality: Commitment to obtaining and creating the finest tea leaves via careful processes to assure consistent high quality
  • • Community Empowerment: Promoting fair trade practices, investing in their development, and ensuring their well-being in order to support and empower local communities
  • • Integrity: Maintaining honesty, openness, and ethical business practices in all facets of the organization's operations
  • • Customer Satisfaction: Meeting the demands of customers by providing great products and experiences that exceed expectations
  • • Education & Awareness: Promoting tea culture by teaching customers on the origins, benefits, and taste of tea, encouraging responsible usage

The company prides itself with internationally recognized certifications of its operations that include Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS), Rainforest Alliance (RA) and Tea Board of Kenya mark of origin.
